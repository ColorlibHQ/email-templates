What shipped this week
Shared canvases.
Sub-issues. A faster editor.
Three of the most-requested features of the year, plus a quiet rewrite that makes the editor 40% faster on long docs. Here's what's new.
▤
Shared canvases
Whiteboard-style canvases that anyone on your team can edit at the same time. With shapes, sticky notes, and live cursors.
⌥
Sub-issues
Break a big issue into smaller ones. Roll up status, completion, and assignees automatically. Up to 10 levels deep.
⚡
40% faster editor
A new incremental renderer means typing latency is gone, even on docs with 10,000+ blocks. No setting to flip — it just works.
